About an Audi R8

The Audi R8 is a mid-engine, 2-seater sports car, which uses Audi's trademark quattro permanent all-wheel drive system. It was introduced by the German car manufacturer Audi AG in 2006.

Unusually for a supercar, the first production Audi R8 has a manual, 6-speed transmission and a silhouette that looks more like a jet plane than a road car. With an rpm of up to 8,250, once heard, the roar from the exhaust is never forgotten.

Price : R800 000
