About a Lamborgini Sports

The Lamborghini car has the reputation of being one of the fastest cars to grace the motor racing world, with the Aventador achieving the Lamborghini top speed of 349 kmph. Lamborghini India imports the cars and sells them off the shelf. The Lamborghini starting price in India is upwards of INR 3 Crores.

Lamborghini engines are all naturally aspirated engines. This means that the engines don't use the force induction of turbochargers or superchargers. The oxygen intake in the engine depends completely on atmospheric pressure. It's an ingenious feat in engineering, physics, and mechanics.
